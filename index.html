HORRORS WAIT INSIDE MOUNT MASSIVE ASYLUM

Acting on a tip from an inside source, independent journalist Miles Upshur breaks into the Mount Massive facility. What he discovers walks a terrifying line between science, religion, and something else entirely. Once inside, his only hope of escape lies with the terrible truth at the heart of Mount Massive Asylum.
